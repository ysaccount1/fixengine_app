Delete Your Data

Take control of your privacy. Learn how to delete your personal data from FixEngine and manage your account preferences.

Back to Home

At FixEngine, we respect your privacy and give you full control over your personal data. You can delete your data at any time through your profile settings in the app or by contacting our support team.

Important: Deleting your data is permanent and cannot be undone. Make sure to export any important diagnostic reports or information before proceeding.

How to Delete Your Data

Method 1: Through Your Profile (Recommended)

The easiest way to delete your data is directly through the FixEngine app:

1

Open the FixEngine App

Launch the FixEngine app on your mobile device and make sure you're signed in to your account.

2

Go to Your Profile

Tap on your profile icon or navigate to the "Profile" section in the app menu.

3

Access Privacy Settings

Look for "Privacy & Data" or "Account Settings" in your profile menu.

4

Select "Delete My Data"

Find and tap on the "Delete My Data" or "Delete Account" option.

5

Confirm Deletion

Review what will be deleted and confirm your decision. You may need to enter your password or verify your identity.

Method 2: Contact Support

If you're unable to access your account or prefer assistance, you can contact our support team:

Email: support@fixengine.app

Subject: Data Deletion Request

Include: Your registered email address and reason for deletion (optional)

What Gets Deleted

When you delete your data, the following information will be permanently removed:

  • Account Information: Email address, profile details, and authentication data
  • Diagnostic Data: All diagnostic reports, notes, and troubleshooting history
  • Uploaded Media: Photos, videos, and other files you've uploaded
  • Usage Data: App usage statistics and preferences
  • Communication History: Support tickets and correspondence

What Happens After Deletion

  • Your account will be immediately deactivated
  • All personal data will be deleted within 30 days
  • You'll receive a confirmation email once deletion is complete
  • You won't be able to recover your data after this process

Data Retention for Legal Compliance

In some cases, we may need to retain certain information for legal or regulatory compliance:

  • Transaction records (for tax and accounting purposes)
  • Legal dispute documentation
  • Fraud prevention records

This data is kept secure and separate from active systems and will be deleted once the retention period expires.

Alternative Options

Before deleting your data completely, consider these alternatives:

  • Export Your Data: Download your diagnostic reports and data before deletion
  • Deactivate Account: Temporarily disable your account without deleting data
  • Adjust Privacy Settings: Limit data collection while keeping your account active

Need Help?

If you have questions about data deletion or need assistance, our support team is here to help.
